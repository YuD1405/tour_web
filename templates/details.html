Tours details

Delve deeper into the specifics of our curated tours. Here, you'll find comprehensive information about each destination, including itinerary highlights, included services, and what to expect during your adventure. Whether you're exploring vibrant cities or tranquil landscapes, our detailed descriptions will help you choose the perfect tour for your interests and preferences. Join us in uncovering the hidden gems and must-see attractions of Vietnam.

  1. Ho Chi Minh City

    Day 1

    • Morning:

      Visit the War Remnants Museum to learn about Vietnam's history and its struggles during the war.

      Breakfast: Traditional Vietnamese pho at a local restaurant.

    • Afternoon:

      Explore the bustling Ben Thanh Market, where you can shop for local goods and souvenirs.

      Lunch: Sample local delicacies at Ben Thanh Market food stalls.

    • Evening:

      Enjoy a dinner cruise on the Saigon River, taking in the beautiful night views of the city.

      Dinner: Onboard the Saigon River dinner cruise.

    Day 2

    • Morning:

      Visit the Cu Chi Tunnels to explore the underground tunnels used during the Vietnam War.

      Breakfast: Banh mi and Vietnamese coffee at a local café.

    • Afternoon:

      Return to the city and visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon and the Saigon Central Post Office.

      Lunch: Vietnamese spring rolls and noodle soup at a local restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Stroll through the Nguyen Hue Walking Street and enjoy the vibrant nightlife.

      Dinner: A fusion meal at a rooftop restaurant with city views.

  2. Da Nang

    Day 1

    • Morning:

      Explore the Marble Mountains, a cluster of five marble and limestone hills.

      Breakfast: Vietnamese pancakes at a local eatery.

    • Afternoon:

      Visit the ancient Cham Museum and learn about the history of the Cham civilization.

      Lunch: Local seafood specialties at a beachside restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Relax on My Khe Beach and enjoy the sunset.

      Dinner: Vietnamese BBQ at a nearby restaurant.

    Day 2

    • Morning:

      Take a day trip to Ba Na Hills and enjoy the Golden Bridge and amusement park.

      Breakfast: Continental breakfast at your hotel.

    • Afternoon:

      Continue exploring Ba Na Hills and its various attractions.

      Lunch: Buffet lunch at Ba Na Hills.

    • Evening:

      Return to Da Nang and stroll along the Han River.

      Dinner: Dinner at a riverside restaurant with a view of the Dragon Bridge.

  3. Ha Noi

    Day 1

    • Morning:

      Visit Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple in the heart of Hanoi.

      Breakfast: Bun cha at a local food stall.

    • Afternoon:

      Explore the Temple of Literature, Vietnam's first national university.

      Lunch: Traditional Vietnamese dishes at a local restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Experience the water puppet show, a unique Vietnamese art form.

      Dinner: Dinner at a traditional Vietnamese restaurant in the Old Quarter.

    Day 2

    • Morning:

      Visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and Presidential Palace.

      Breakfast: Pho at a popular local spot.

    • Afternoon:

      Explore the bustling streets of the Old Quarter and shop for souvenirs.

      Lunch: Hanoi street food tour.

    • Evening:

      Enjoy the nightlife in Hanoi with a walk through the night market.

      Dinner: Dinner at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the city.

  4. Phu Quoc

    Day 1

    • Morning:

      Relax on the beautiful Sao Beach.

      Breakfast: Continental breakfast at your resort.

    • Afternoon:

      Explore the Phu Quoc Prison Museum to learn about its history.

      Lunch: Fresh seafood at a beachside restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Visit the Dinh Cau Night Market for shopping and local food.

      Dinner: Dinner at the night market, trying various local dishes.

    Day 2

    • Morning:

      Snorkeling and diving tour in the An Thoi Archipelago.

      Breakfast: Buffet breakfast at your resort.

    • Afternoon:

      Continue exploring the marine life and relaxing on the boat.

      Lunch: Lunch on the boat with freshly caught seafood.

    • Evening:

      Relax at your resort and enjoy a spa session.

      Dinner: Fine dining at your resort's restaurant.

  5. Da Lat

    Day 1

    • Morning:

      Visit the Valley of Love, a picturesque park with beautiful landscapes.

      Breakfast: Vietnamese coffee and pastries at a local café.

    • Afternoon:

      Explore the Da Lat Flower Gardens and take in the vibrant floral displays.

      Lunch: Local specialties at a nearby restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Stroll around Xuan Huong Lake and enjoy the cool evening breeze.

      Dinner: Hot pot dinner at a popular local restaurant.

    Day 2

    • Morning:

      Visit the Crazy House, a unique architectural marvel in Da Lat.

      Breakfast: Traditional Vietnamese breakfast at a local café.

    • Afternoon:

      Take a cable car ride to Truc Lam Pagoda and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

      Lunch: Vegetarian meal at the pagoda's restaurant.

    • Evening:

      Explore the Da Lat Night Market for local crafts and street food.

      Dinner: Street food at the night market, trying various local delicacies.
